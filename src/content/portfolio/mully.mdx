## Overview

    Mully is a personal project aimed at designing and developing a modern digital banking platform. The objective was to create a seamless, secure, and user-friendly banking experience. The project focused on balancing a sleek design aesthetic with essential features like account management, transaction history, and financial insights, catering to both tech-savvy and general banking users.
